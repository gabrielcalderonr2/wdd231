Website Plan - DriveSure Motors

Site Name

DriveSure Motors

This name conveys reliability and mobility. It’s professional, memorable, and fits the automotive industry.
Optional domain: drivesuremotors.com

Site Purpose

DriveSure Motors provides a digital platform to browse and shop for quality used cars. Visitors can view detailed listings, search/filter by car type, explore financing options, read car buying tips, and schedule test drives.

Scenarios

  • Where can I find an affordable SUV with low mileage near me?
  • What financing options are available for first-time car buyers?
  • How can I schedule a test drive for a listed vehicle?

Color Schema

Midnight Blue (#003366) – Navigation, header, footer
Bright Orange (#FF6600) – Buttons, highlights
Light Gray (#F4F4F4) – Background, cards

Typography

  • Montserrat (sans-serif): Used for all headings and buttons
  • Open Sans (sans-serif): Used for paragraph text and navigation
